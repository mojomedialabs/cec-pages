At CEC, safety is more than just a number or a measurement — it’s a core principal, starting with a constant focus on training and safety procedures. Our company strongly believes that an informed employee is the best defense against any job-related accident. Our new employees undergo an extensive safety orientation before ever starting work and receive additional site-specific safety training on hazards unique to each project.

CEC’s safety training programs include:

  • "Hot Work"
  • Lockout/Tagout
  • Trenching/excavation
  • HAZCOM
  • OSHA 10-hour and 30-hour training
  • CPR/First Aid
  • Forklift Certification
  • Aerial Lift Training
  • Confined Space Rescue
  • Fall Protection
  • Powder actuated certifications
  • MSDS Data Sheets
