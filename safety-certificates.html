At CEC, safety is more than just a number or a measurement — it’s a mindset, starting with a constant focus on training and procedures. Our company strongly believes that an aware employee is the best defense against any job-related accident. Our new employees undergo an extensive safety orientation before starting work and receive site-specific safety training on hazards unique to each project.

CEC’s safety training programs include:

  • Extensive New Hire Orientation
  • Behavioral Based Training
  • "Hot Work"
  • Lockout/Tagout
  • Trenching/excavation
  • HAZCOM
  • OSHA 10-hour and 30-hour training
  • CPR/First Aid
  • Forklift Certification
  • Aerial Lift Training
  • Confined Space Rescue
  • Fall Protection
  • Powder actuated certifications
  • MSDS Data Sheets

CEC’s safety program consistently outperforms national averages for EMR, lost work time, and incidence rates. We have a strong commitment to provide a safe and secure work environment.

Our Safety Team is focused and proactive on Accident Prevention and providing Behavioral based training. CEC is proud to have a fully functional Safety Committee that involves all employment positions of CEC from the management to the helper in efforts to uncover the root cause of all safety, health and risk related issues and develop mechanisms (programs) to keep our employees safe.
